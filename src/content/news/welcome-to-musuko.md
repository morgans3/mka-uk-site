We're thrilled to announce the launch of the Musuko Karate Academy website! This is your new home for all things Musuko — from class schedules and instructor profiles to news and updates from the club.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Class information** — Check our timetable and find a session that works for you
- **Meet the team** — Learn about our experienced instructors
- **Club products** — Browse the equipment and merchandise available
- **Latest news** — Stay up to date with gradings, events, and club announcements

## New Members Welcome

Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced karateka looking for a new club, you're welcome at Musuko. Your first class is always free, so come along and give it a try.

We look forward to seeing you on the mat!
