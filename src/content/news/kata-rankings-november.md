We're incredibly proud to announce that Musuko Karate Academy has been ranked **number one in the country** in the November 2025 national kata rankings.

Our students collected an outstanding **7 gold, 8 silver, and 4 bronze** medals across competitions, placing us ahead of clubs from across England, Scotland, and Northern Ireland.

## The Rankings

The November 2025 kata leaderboard sees Musuko at the top, ahead of Worcestershire Karate Squad in second and Scottish Karate Federation in third. This is a tremendous achievement for a club of our size and reflects the hard work and dedication of every student and coach.

## Thank You

This result belongs to every student who has trained hard, every parent who has supported them, and every instructor who has given their time. We couldn't be prouder of what this club has achieved together.

Let's keep pushing forward — the best is yet to come!
